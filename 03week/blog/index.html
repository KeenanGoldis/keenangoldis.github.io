Why the Sausage is King

The sausage is king because all lesser meats must bow down before it! Only the sausage has the flavor of many mysterious animals, rather than just one measly species!

Fancy steaks have fancy marbling. And it will cost you $12 per lb at the HEB. Sausage, on the other hand, has perfectly equal distribution of the 'marble', at a price of about $2-$3 per lb.

To smoke a brisket, you must smoke for 10-16 hours. To smoke ribs, you must smoke for 3-6 hours. But sausage, that will only take 1-3 hours MAX!

So You Want to Open Up a Food Trailer, eh?

Opening up a food trailer can be a great way to test out a business concept, but it is by no means an easy endeavor. It is both (relative to other businesses) easy and cheap to get started, which in turn make it a very competitive market.

A great question to ask yourself woud be: Do I want to be chef, or do I want to own a business? If you answer that you want to be a chef, then owning a food trailer can be a great opportunity for you!

If, however, you want to be a business owner more than a chef, I would suggest proceeding with caution. It is very likely that you will be PERSONALLY cooking everyday in the food trailer, so if the idea of 'working the line' doesn't excite you, then you may be better off with a different business concept.
